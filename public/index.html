Welcome to Verus.

Verus is a veteran AI builder program created by Sanōva AI and launching with a pilot cohort at Patriots’ Hall.

We help veterans move beyond the chat and into practical tools they can build, deploy, and own — whether they are transitioning, building a business, or learning how AI fits into civilian work.

This is hands-on, respectful, and outcome-driven. Not a certificate. Not a login to someone else’s platform. Real infrastructure they build and control.
